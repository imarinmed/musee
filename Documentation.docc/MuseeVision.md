# Musee's Vision

## Democratizing Beauty Analysis

Musee was born from the belief that beauty analysis should be objective, scientific, and accessible to everyone. In an era of subjective social media standards and filtered imagery, Musee provides a mathematical foundation for understanding aesthetic appeal grounded in extensive research through 2026.

## The Science of Attractiveness

### Evidence-Based Beauty Assessment

Beauty quantification has evolved significantly beyond ancient mathematical principles:

#### Classical Foundations
- **Golden Ratio (Ï† = 1.618)**: Found in nature and classical art, but with limited universal validity (35-45% population adherence)
- **Fibonacci Sequence**: Underlying pattern in attractive proportions
- **Symmetry**: Bilateral balance strongly correlates with perceived health (r = -0.3 to -0.5)
- **Harmony**: Balanced relationships between facial features

#### Modern Research Integration (2026)
Musee incorporates findings from extensive contemporary research:
- **Neoclassical Canons**: Ancient Greek proportional rules (55-65% population deviation)
- **Facial Thirds**: Vertical division balance (often unequal in natural faces)
- **Cross-Cultural Standards**: Ethnic-specific beauty ideals with 91-93% consistency in overall preferences
- **Longitudinal Analysis**: Beauty evolution tracking with trend analysis
- **Skin Quality**: Primary visual impact factor (texture, tone, radiance)
- **Feature-Specific Metrics**: 16+ detailed measurements for eyes, nose, mouth
